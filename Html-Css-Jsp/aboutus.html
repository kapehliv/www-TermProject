Welcome to "Cook it"!

"Cook it" is a web-application, which helps you to find easily and quickly recipes based on your ingredients you have in your shelves.
You have to check the ingredients you have and let "Cook it" display a variety of recipes in three categories:
Recipes that match exactly with your ingredients,
Recipes that can be cooked with less ingredients, so you can save some of them!,
or informs you that you should go shopping, because you don't have many ingedients for cooking!

Don't be afraid to search recipes based on your wine!

Thank you very much for visiting our site!
